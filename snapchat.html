How does that relate to Education

Considering Snapchat is all around the world and is used by all age groups mostly teens and young adults. It presents an opportunity for schools and colleges/universities to use this to develop and progress work in education. As more people spend time on their phones this would help students and teachers if they somehow made it possible to transfer or work from devices mainly secondary schools. Also taking screenshots and passing reminders to students or even classmates would prove really eficient as teachers should connect with how the modern world works now and take advantage of it.


How to install

How to install Snapchat onto your phone.


  1. Download the Snapchat Application in the App Store. Go to the app store on your device, click on the search button, and type in Snapchat.
  2. Scroll until you find the app, select it, and click install.
  3. Launch the Snapchat App and Tap the Snapchat icon (a small ghost) from your device home screen.
